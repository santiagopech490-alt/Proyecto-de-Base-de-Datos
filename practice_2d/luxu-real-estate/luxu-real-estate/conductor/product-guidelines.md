# Product Guidelines - Luxu Real Estate

## Brand Voice and Tone
The brand voice of Luxu Real Estate is **Sophisticated & High-End**. Every communication should reflect elegance, exclusivity, and a premium quality that resonates with our target audience of luxury home buyers.

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity & Precision:** Every design element must be purposeful and organized, ensuring that the interface is easy to understand and use.
2. **Exclusivity:** The visual language should consistently convey a high-end, premium feel.
3. **Property First:** The design must prioritize the properties themselves, using layout and typography to enhance their presentation.

## UI Components and Style
- **Large Visual Elements:** High-quality, high-resolution property images and videos should be the focal point of the UI, allowing the properties to speak for themselves.
- **Typography:** Use premium, highly legible fonts to reinforce the sophisticated brand identity.
- **Whitespace:** Use whitespace intentionally to create a sense of openness and luxury.

## UX Patterns
- **Progressive Disclosure:** Present users with essential information first, allowing them to dive deeper into property details, floor plans, and amenities only when they express interest. This keeps the interface clean and focused.
- **Seamless Discovery:** Ensure the journey from discovery to property details is smooth and intuitive.
- **Premium Interactions:** Every interaction, from page transitions to micro-interactions, should feel refined and intentional.
